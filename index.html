Who We Are

The BCC is a registered not for profit organization dedicated to the protection, promotion and support of breastfeeding and the implementation of the Baby-Friendly Initiative (BFI) in Canada.

Learn more

Baby-Friendly Initiative (BFI)

Officially called the BFHI Network for Industrialized Nations, Central and Eastern European Nations and Independent States, the BFHI Network was established in 1997 in coordination with the WHO, by BFHI Country Coordinators and focal points from Industrialized Nations, Central and Eastern European Countries and Independent States. Its has active participation from 41 countries.
